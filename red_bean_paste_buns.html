Red Bean Paste Bun : Spirited Away

Pillow soft steamed red bean buns(豆沙包) is one of the most popular Chinese dessert.

Those lovely and soft steamed buns are so great for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 2/5 cup of all purpose flour
  • 40g sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar tolerant (instant) yeast
  • 180 ml warm milk
  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • pinch of salt
  • 200 g red bean past filling

Method

  • Get a pot and put it on low, low heat

  • Add the milk and use a thermometer to heat the milk to 35 C

  • Add to a large enough bowl with the flour, sugar, yeast, veggie oil, and salt.

  • Mix this around with a spoon or so (or a mixer with paddles for kneading on) before switching to your hands to knead the dough

  • Get everything worked in nice and good before setting it in a room temperature area for an hour for the dough to rise.

  • After an hour, add like a tablespoon or 2 more of the flour and knead some more

  • Spoon the red bean paste into the center and close it up nice and tight.

  • Flip these over and shape into a dome as much as you can

  • Line the steamer bowl with parchment paper

  • When they are done steaming, turn off the steamer and leave them in there for about 5 more minutes
